Frequently Asked Questions

What happened to the previous version of the GeneDB website?

The old GeneDB website was replaced in January 2019. We are providing a new GeneDB website that is based on information publicly available on Wikidata.

How do I cite GeneDB?

Please cite the 2012 GeneDB publication, and/or the 2019 GeneDB and Wikidata one.

Where can I view annotation statistics?

We provide genome GFF files for all of the curated species here, which are updated on a monthly basis.
