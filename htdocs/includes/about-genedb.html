About GeneDB

The primary goal of GeneDB is to provide access to genome annotation for species that are undergoing manual curation and refinement.

The site is updated monthly to reflect close to real-time changes in annotation, before they have been propagated across EuPathDB and other sites with whom we collaborate.

Annotations reflect changes made in-house by scientists, as part of their research projects, and by dedicated curators based in-house and in collaborating organisations.
